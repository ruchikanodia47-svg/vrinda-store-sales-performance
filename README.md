#  Vrinda Store Sales Performance Dashboard

An interactive retail sales dashboard created in Microsoft Excel to track annual revenue, fulfillment channels, customer demographics, and regional order distributions for Vrinda Store. 

This project showcases end-to-end data analysis, including data transformation, pivot tracking, and user-centric visualization design.

---

###  Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) Captured
* **Total Revenue:** ₹2,11,76,377
* **Total Orders Placed:** 31,047 orders
* **Average Order Value:** ₹682.07
* **Total Quantity Sold:** 31,237 items
* **Top Selling Channel:** Amazon

---

###  Data Insights & Metrics
* **Channel Distribution:** **Amazon** dominates the store's revenue generation (₹75,19,933), followed by **Myntra** (Sub-₹50L) and **Flipkart** (Sub-₹46L). 
* **Regional & City Leaders:** **Maharashtra** is the leading state by volume with 4,519 orders, with **Bengaluru** ranking as the top individual city yielding 2,673 orders.
* **Demographic Breakdown:** The **Adult** age group drives the vast majority of sales, contributing over ₹1.06 Crore to the total revenue.
* **Monthly Trends:** Revenue peaked early in the year during **March** at ₹19,28,066, before experiencing a gradual decline toward the winter months, hitting a low in November.
* **Size Preferences:** Standard clothing sizes **M (Medium)** and **L (Large)** are the most sought-after by customers, reflecting the highest quantity sold metrics.

---

###  Features & Tools Used
* **Data Aggregation:** Leveraged Excel Pivot Tables to isolate complex demographic breakdowns (Age Group vs. Revenue, Size Preferences).
* **Interactive Filtering:** Integrated multi-slicer tools filtering dynamically across *Category* (e.g., Kurta, Saree, Western Dress), *Channels*, *Age Groups*, and *Order Dates*.
* **Advanced Charting:** Utilized mixed visualization techniques including line charts for seasonal trend analysis and horizontal bar charts for geographic order tracking.
* **Design & UX:** Implemented a unified purple-palette aesthetic with customized icon badges for intuitive data consumption.
